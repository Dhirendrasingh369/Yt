How Does an Online YouTube Downloader Work?

When you paste a YouTube URL into YTGrab, our server uses the powerful open-source yt-dlp library to query YouTube's internal streaming API and extract all available video and audio streams. YouTube hosts every video in multiple formats and quality levels. YTGrab fetches all options and lets you pick the format that suits your needs.

Once you choose a format and click download, our server processes the video file and streams it directly to your browser in real time. Files are never permanently stored on our servers — they are deleted immediately after delivery to protect your privacy.

YouTube to MP4 — Best Quality Video Download

MP4 is the most universally compatible video format, supported by every device including iPhones, Android phones, Windows PCs, Macs, smart TVs, and tablets. When you download a YouTube video as MP4, you get:
